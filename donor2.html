Donate Plasma for Money - Get Rewarded for Your Time.
BioLife plasma donors spend up to an hour and a half, as often as twice a week, in our centers to help save someone's life or improve the quality of it. Plasma cannot be produced synthetically. It can only be obtained from healthy, eligible adults; therefore we offer compensation to our donors for their commitment to the program.

For specific information on compensation, please contact your local center.
To get started donating plasma for money, locate a plasma donation center near you to schedule your plasma donation.


At BioLife, we think it’s pretty awesome that you’re choosing to spend your time donating plasma. Since plasma can’t be made in a lab, people with rare and complex chronic diseases rely on donors like you to live healthier and more full lives. Knowing you’re making a vital impact in others’ lives is the best feeling in the world. And getting paid for your time is just the cherry on top.


With the BioLife Debit Card. Every dollar you receive from donating plasma will automatically be loaded onto this card. Your BioLife Debit Card is a Mastercard that can be used to make purchases wherever Debit Mastercard is accepted and at over 900,000 ATM locations worldwide.

Donating plasma takes more time than donating blood. You will spend roughly 1-2 hours at our center to complete your donation, so we want to thank you for dedicating this amount of time with us. Taking the time to donate plasma is a commitment but a generous one! For that, we want to reward you.
Your donation is vital in the production of life-changing treatments and therapies for people who suffer from rare or complex chronic diseases. Just two donations of plasma could help make a huge difference to someone who relies on plasma therapy.


When you refer a friend to donate plasma at BioLife, you could double the impact for people who depend on plasma therapies. You could also earn a buddy bonus that gets deposited on your BioLife card. The more friends you refer, the more good you can do and the more you could earn.
